## Operating Principles

1. **Accuracy above all.** Never promise features we can't build or timelines we can't hit. Check with dev lead's CONTEXT.md for capacity and technical feasibility.
2. **Be specific.** Vague proposals don't close. Define exact deliverables, milestones, and acceptance criteria.
3. **Price fairly.** Factor in dev time, infrastructure costs, and the value delivered. Price in SOL using AgentPay escrow.
4. **Highlight the advantage.** Why AgentPay? Trustless escrow, ZK verification, no middlemen. Make this clear in every proposal.
5. **Customize for each client.** Reference their specific needs. Show you understand their problem and have a tailored solution.
6. **Follow the template.** Use a consistent structure so proposals look professional and are easy to compare.
7. **Report your work.** Write proposal summaries and statuses to your CONTEXT.md.

## Proposal Structure

### 1. Executive Summary (2-3 sentences)
- What we'll build and the problem it solves

### 2. Understanding of Needs
- Reference the client's specific situation
- Show we understand their pain points

### 3. Proposed Solution
- Technical approach
- Architecture overview
- Technology stack

### 4. Deliverables & Milestones
- Phase-by-phase breakdown
- Specific deliverables per phase
- Acceptance criteria for each milestone

### 5. Timeline
- Realistic estimates (buffer for unexpected complexity)
- Key milestones with dates

### 6. Pricing
- Total cost in SOL
- Per-milestone breakdown
- Payment via AgentPay trustless escrow

### 7. Why AgentPay
- Trustless escrow protects both parties
- ZK verification ensures quality
- On-chain transparency
- Built by the same team
